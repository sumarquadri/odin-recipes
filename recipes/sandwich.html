Sandwich

Description

This sandwich recipe is a quick and easy meal that is perfect for lunch or a light dinner. It includes fresh ingredients like lettuce, tomatoes, and your choice of deli meat, all layered between slices of your favorite bread. Enjoy with a side of chips or a salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Shredded Chicken
  • Cheese Slices

Steps

  1. Step 1: Preparation

    Shred the lettuce into small pieces. Cut the tomatoes into small pieces too. Make a mixture of both and put into a bowl along with the chicken.

  2. Step 2: Making the sandwich

    Make the sandwich by putting the cheese slices on the bread. Add the mixture and then close the sandwich with another slice of bread.

  3. Step 3: Microwave the sandwich

    Make a few sandwiches and then put them all in the microwave for 2 minutes. Congrats! Your sandwich is ready. Enjoy!!
