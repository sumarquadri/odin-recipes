Pancakes

Description

Fluffy and delicious pancakes are the perfect breakfast treat. Serve them with syrup, fresh fruits, or whipped cream for a delightful start to your day.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Step 1: Prepare the batter

    Mix flour, sugar, milk, and eggs in a bowl. Whisk until smooth.

  2. Step 2: Cook the pancakes

    Heat a pan and add butter. Pour small amounts of batter to form pancakes. Cook until golden brown on both sides.

  3. Step 3: Serve and enjoy

    Stack the pancakes and serve with your favorite toppings.
