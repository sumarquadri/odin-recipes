Pasta

Description

This pasta recipe is a simple and delicious meal, perfect for a quick lunch or dinner. Made with fresh tomatoes, garlic, and your favorite type of pasta, it’s both nutritious and satisfying.

Ingredients

  • Pasta of your choice
  • Tomatoes
  • Garlic
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese

Steps

  1. Step 1: Cook the pasta

    Boil water in a pot and cook the pasta as per the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

  2. Step 2: Prepare the sauce

    Chop tomatoes and garlic. Sauté them in olive oil for a few minutes until softened.

  3. Step 3: Mix and serve

    Combine the pasta with the sauce. Sprinkle Parmesan cheese on top and serve hot.
