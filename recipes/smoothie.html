Smoothie

Description

This smoothie recipe is a refreshing and healthy drink, perfect for a quick breakfast or snack. Packed with fruits and nutrients, it's both delicious and energizing.

Ingredients

  • Banana
  • Strawberries
  • Yogurt
  • Milk
  • Honey

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather ingredients

    Peel the banana and wash the strawberries. Cut them into small pieces.

  2. Step 2: Blend

    Put all the ingredients into a blender. Blend until smooth.

  3. Step 3: Serve

    Pour into a glass and enjoy immediately.
